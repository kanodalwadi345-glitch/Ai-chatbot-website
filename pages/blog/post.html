Designing with Glow

Use neon carefully: focus accents, avoid full neon backgrounds, and prefer soft gradients.

Key Points

  • Limit bright hues to accents and borders.
  • Blend glassmorphism with subtle shadows.
  • Respect accessibility: keep contrast high.
